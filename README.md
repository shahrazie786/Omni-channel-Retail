# Omni-channel-Retail
Project Details:
**Project Name: Omnichannel Retail Sales and Inventory Analytics Dashboard**
**Executive Problem Statement**
In the rapidly digitizing retail landscape, offline businesses transitioning to digital models
frequently encounter systemic bottlenecks due to fragmented data across multiple sales
channels. As multi-location retailers scale operations, they struggle to unify data from
brick-and-mortar Point of Sale (POS) systems and online storefronts. This lack of centralized
data leads to an inability to track overall sales trends, forecast inventory requirements, and
identify top-performing product categories.
The objective of this project is to develop an end-to-end Data Analytics pipeline and an
interactive Business Intelligence dashboard that unifies physical and digital retail data. The
platform must process raw sales transactions, identify seasonal revenue patterns, and provide
actionable business intelligence to optimize inventory turnover and targeted marketing.
**Business Objectives and Key Performance Indicators**
To ensure the solution delivers tangible enterprise value, the analysis must be oriented around
specific operational metrics. The primary objective is to uncover hidden sales trends, calculate
essential revenue metrics like Total Sales and Average Revenue per Order, and identify the
peak purchasing periods throughout the day and week.
Data integrity is paramount. The intern must eliminate discrepancies by thoroughly cleaning the
raw transaction logs, handling missing values, and standardizing data formats. The success of
the project will be quantified by the dashboard's ability to seamlessly filter data across different
timeframes (e.g., YoY, MoM growth), locations, and product categories to enable data-driven
decision-making for inventory managers.
**User Personas and Operational Workflows**
**1. Store Manager**
**Primary Operational Needs** 
Localized sales insights, daily performance tracking, and identifying fast-moving inventory items.
**System Interaction and Workflow**
Uses the dashboard to monitor daily revenue, compare weekday vs. weekend sales performance,and track specific product category trends.

**2. Inventory Planner**
**Primary Operational Needs** 
Macroscopic visibility of stock movement, seasonality forecasting, and identifying low-performing products to minimize dead stock.
**System Interaction and Workflow**
Analyzes historical data to predict Q4 holiday spikes and adjusts supply chain procurement strategies to prevent stockouts.

**3. Regional Sales Director**
**Primary Operational Needs** 
High-level revenue aggregations, geographic performance comparisons, and ROI analysis.
**System Interaction and Workflow**
Reviews interactive dashboards to evaluate the financial health of multiple retail nodes and makes overarching pricing strategy decisions.
